      Help & Workflow Guide

      How GovTracker works

      1. Found — SAM.gov sync discovers matching opportunities.
      2. Analyzed — Claude screens each contract and writes a plain-English summary.
      3. Subs — Find subcontractors, collect quotes, complete the pre-bid checklist.
      4. Proposal — Configure margin, generate proposal, complete submission checklist.
      5. Submitted — Track submission and, after award, move to Performance.

      Dashboard cards

      Each card shows score, due date, workflow progress, and one primary action. Click the card to open full details. Use filters to focus on contracts that need attention.

      After award

      When a contract is awarded, the Performance tab appears on the contract detail page. Track monthly invoices, sub payments, option years, and CPARS ratings from the Performance nav item.
